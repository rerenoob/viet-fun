🔊 Pronunciation Help

How Pronunciation Works

🎯 Tap to Hear

Tap any Vietnamese word in the app to hear it pronounced!

Look for the 🔊 button or underlined Vietnamese text.

🔧 Voice Setup

The app uses your device's built-in text-to-speech voices.

If Vietnamese doesn't sound right:

  • Your device might need Vietnamese language pack
  • Try installing Vietnamese TTS voice in system settings
  • On iPhone/iPad: Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices
  • On Android: Settings → Language & input → Text-to-speech

🎵 Tone Explorer

Vietnamese has 6 tones that change word meaning:

ma (ghost) · mà (but) · má (mom) · mả (grave) · mã (horse) · mạ (rice seedling)

Each tone has a different pitch pattern. Tap cards in Tone Explorer to hear them!
